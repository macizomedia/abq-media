# Deep Research Prompt (Sample)

Topic: Venezuela oil sanctions update and geopolitical implications

Goal:
- Identify recent policy shifts, key stakeholders, and second-order effects
- Focus on regional impact, energy markets, and political leverage

Questions to answer:
- What changed in the last 30-90 days?
- Who benefits, who loses, and why?
- What are plausible next moves by regional actors?

Deliverables:
- 5 key claims with sources to verify
- 3 counterpoints or uncertainties
- Suggested framing for a briefing-style podcast episode
